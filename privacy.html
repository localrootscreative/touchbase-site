Privacy Policy

Touchbase respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Contacts you choose to add or import
  • Check-in dates, reminders, and optional notes
  • Notification preferences

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to provide the Touchbase service — organizing relationships and sending reminders you opt into.

What We Do NOT Do

  • No auto-messaging contacts
  • No reading message content
  • No selling or sharing your data

Data Storage

Data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud) with user-scoped access controls.

Your Control

You may delete contacts or your account at any time from within the app.

Contact

Questions? Email: support@touchbase.app (or your preferred email)
